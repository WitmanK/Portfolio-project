Spain

In January 2023 we have travelled to a small town in the North of Spain. We stayed in Gijón for 4 nights, and the total cost for the flights and accomondation was £55pp.

Gijón Surf Hostel
One of the reasons why the trip was so cheap, was because we decided to not spend much on the accomodation. We stayed in a surf hostel in a small surfing town, Gijón. The facilities were very clean, staff extremely friendly and overall it was a positive stay. The hostel offers trips/ equipment for surfing, paddle boarding and more. The total cost per person/ 4 night was £40.

Jesters of Arenillas
The time spent in Asturias was interesting to say the least. Without any preparation we have booked flight tickets in January (no wonder why they were so cheap!). Our first day in Asturias we decided to visit Jesters of Arenillas. Beautiful natural formation of clifs that with the incoming waves, the holes in the cliff would explode with sea water. Pleasent views with a small adrenaline rush.

Night life
During winter times in north of Spain, ther is not much nightlife. You can't find any bars that are open before 5PM, and you're limited with drinks selection. There is a lot of beauty in the simplicity of this. We have spent much time in local bar/ restaurants, speaking to locals and of course, polish up on our Spanish (as there is a small percentage of people that speak English in the region).
